Aurova — Request Data Deletion

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of data associated with the Aurova: Live Photo Wallpaper app, published by developer digitals85.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to support@aurovaapp.com.
  2. Use the subject line "Aurova data deletion request."
  3. In the message, state that you would like your Aurova data deleted. No account is required — you have no username with us, so simply include any detail that helps us locate your records (for example, the approximate date you installed the app). You may attach a screenshot of the app if helpful.
We will confirm and complete the deletion within 30 days of receiving your request.

What data is deleted

On request, we delete the diagnostic and usage data associated with your app installation, which is the only data Aurova collects:

  • App interaction / usage analytics (Google Firebase Analytics)
  • Crash logs and diagnostics (Google Firebase Crashlytics)
  • The device / analytics identifier tied to the above

What is not stored (nothing to delete)

Your photos never leave your device — Aurova reads and renders them entirely on-device, so we never receive, store, or transmit them. There is no photo data on our side to delete. Removing the app from your device removes all on-device photo processing.

What may be retained

We may retain minimal records required to comply with legal obligations or to confirm that a deletion request was honored. Aggregated, anonymized statistics that cannot be linked back to you may also be retained.
