Aurova — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Aurova (“the app”) is a live-wallpaper app. This policy explains what data the app handles.

Your photos

Photos you add are read from your device, processed, and rendered entirely on your device to display the wallpaper. They are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared.

Information we collect

We use Google Firebase to keep the app stable and understand general usage:

  • Crash diagnostics (Firebase Crashlytics) — stack traces and device/OS details when the app crashes.
  • Aggregate usage analytics (Firebase Analytics) — anonymous, aggregated events and a device-level analytics identifier.

We do not collect your name, email, contacts, location, or the content of your photos.

Purchases

The one-time unlock is processed by Google Play Billing. We never see or store your payment details.

Sharing & selling

We do not sell your data and do not share it with third parties except the Google services above, which process it on our behalf.

Data retention & deletion

Analytics and crash data are retained per Google Firebase defaults. To request deletion, email mitcho85@gmail.com.

Children

Aurova is not directed at children under 13.
